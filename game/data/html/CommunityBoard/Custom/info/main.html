Server Info
Leveling up From level 1 there are chain quests for every class, in order to level up until Awakening at Lv. 85.

Farm Zones There are multiple farm zones in which you can obtain coins for R-grade equipment and level up.

Equipment The equipment until 85 is free. From R-grade to R99, there is basic equip available.

Upgrade System You can upgrade R and R99 equipment into blessed talking with the npcs in giran. The R99 equipment has more upgrades.
